Rolex is a renowned luxury watch manufacturer founded in 1905 by Hans Wilsdorf and Alfred Davis. With a legacy spanning over a century, Rolex is synonymous with precision, elegance, and craftsmanship. The brand is celebrated for its timeless designs, innovative technology, and unwavering commitment to excellence.
